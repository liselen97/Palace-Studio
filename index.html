WHO WE ARE

The Palace Dance Studio (TPDS) is where dancers can learn from some of the leading choreographers in New Zealand. We are a Hip Hop Dance studio, catering for all ages and all levels of Hip Hop Dance.The Palace Dance Studio is where you can learn, rehearse, create, collaborate and grow. Our studio promotes a supportive, smoke-free, alcohol-free, no swearing, friendly environment.
